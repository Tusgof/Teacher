# Mission: Understand 0DTE Market Microstructure Papers

## Why
Build enough practical understanding to read empirical market microstructure papers on 0DTE options, explain their causal claims, and judge whether the evidence is useful for investment research or trading hypotheses.

## Success looks like
- Explain the paper's main claim in plain language without losing the mechanism.
- Distinguish between 0DTE trading volume, market maker inventory, net gamma, and hedging flow.
- Read the identification strategy and know what the paper can and cannot claim.
- Convert the paper into testable research hypotheses.

## Constraints
- Keep lessons short and directly tied to the paper being read.
- Prefer concrete intuition before equations.
- Use the local paper file as the primary source unless the user asks for outside sources.

## Out of scope
- Full option pricing derivations.
- Live trading recommendations from the paper alone.
