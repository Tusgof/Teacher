# Mission

Teach `Regime-Conditional Alpha in SPY 0DTE Opening Range Breakout Strategies` from zero in a self-contained way.

The learner should be able to explain what a SPY 0DTE debit vertical spread is, what an opening range breakout signal is, why the paper filters by day of week, VIX regime, and macro events, why strike distance changes the economics of the trade, why the paper concludes the edge is marginal rather than robust, and what limitations follow from using a simulation instead of observed live fills.
