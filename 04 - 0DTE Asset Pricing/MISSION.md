# Mission

Teach `0DTE Asset Pricing` from zero in a self-contained way.

The learner should be able to explain what asset pricing asks in plain language, why option prices reveal what investors pay to insure against states of the market, what physical and risk-neutral distributions mean, what a pricing kernel is without relying on formulas, why the paper finds unusually high compensation for upside risk in 0DTE options, and what SSD bound violations mean.
