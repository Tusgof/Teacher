# Mission: Understand 0DTE Trading Rules Papers

## Why
Build enough practical understanding to read empirical 0DTE payoff and trading-rule papers, distinguish unconditional option premia from implementable strategy performance, and judge whether a reported trading rule is robust enough to become a research hypothesis.

## Success looks like
- Explain why a positive 0DTE variance risk premium does not automatically become an easy trading edge.
- Distinguish payoff, PNL, return, execution friction, tail risk, and out-of-sample performance.
- Read the paper's tables without confusing unconditional averages with conditional rules.
- Convert the paper into guarded research hypotheses for further testing.

## Constraints
- Keep lessons short and directly tied to the paper.
- Prefer concrete payoff intuition before formulas.
- Use the local paper file as the primary source.

## Out of scope
- Live trading recommendations.
- Full derivation of VIX methodology or option pricing theory.
